ABOUT

Over the last 10 years, Andrew Thiele has become known as the founder of Artek, a thriving creative agency based in midtown Manhattan. More recently, Thiele is recognized for his large-scale paintings that redefine culture and cross socio-economic borders from 5th avenue-advertising to lost signage in the Bronx. The prolific artist leads his audience through intimate views of urbanization and pop culture.

STATEMENT

Someone once asked me"why" Why do I make art... Wheather it be artwork to represent thier brand with my agency or artwork I make myself from my soul. I am constantly creating. Its who I am and why I am here. Art is part of my existence.
