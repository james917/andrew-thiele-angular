Over the last 10 years, Andrew Thiele has become known
as the founder of Artek, a thriving creative agency based in midtown
Manhattan. More recently, Thiele is recognized for his large-scale paintings
that redefine culture and cross socio-economic borders from 5th
avenue advertising to lost signage in the Bronx. The prolific outsider artist
leads his audience through intimate views of urbanization and pop culture.
